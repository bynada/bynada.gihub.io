Late Shipment Analysis

Background: Investigated delivery delays to identify operational and regional factors impacting on-time performance (OTD%).

Problem: Frequent late deliveries and inconsistent shipping performance reduced customer satisfaction and hindered logistics planning.

Method: Performed EDA and regression modeling in Python to quantify how shipment features influence delay likelihood.

Output: Identified key predictors (e.g., carrier, destination region) and delivered an insight-focused dashboard to guide improvements in OTD tracking.
